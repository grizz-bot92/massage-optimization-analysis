# Massage-optimization-analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes historical appointment data from a massage therapy business to identify patterns in no-shows, utilization, and service demand. The goal is to uncover actionable insights that could improve scheduling efficiency and revenue.

## Business Questions

  - When are no-shows most likely to occur?
  - Which appointment times and services are underutilized?
  - Are certain services or time slots more efficinet than others?

## Data Collection

  - **Source**: The data was extracted from a PostgreSQL database.
  -  **Data Points**: Massage practice  data on client demographics, appointment types, service durations, appointment cost, and scheduling patterns.
  -  **Data Scope**: This analysis uses appointment data from 2021-2023 and partial data from 2024(January-October). 2024 data is used for trend ananlysis and not directl comparable for full year totals.
